Micaela Tucker

Musician, Writer, Artist

Micaela stopped writing her memoir when she realized a book required an “about the author” section. The direct correlation of a memoir being a book about the author was lost on her. She inherited her feet and belly laugh from her father. She inherited her cheek bones and eating with her hands from her mother. She believes in the Christ “in whom we live and move and have our being.” She followed a whim of creativity and asked for funds through an indiegogo campaign. Thanks to her generous contributors, her debut album “good place to be when you don’t know where you are” is now streaming. Please inquire to collaborate. She’d love to create with you!
